Structural Design

2018-10-01

We started with a few exercises regarding the drawing of internal actions diagrams.

Trusses

Trusses, either 2D or 3D, are developed following the concept of the rigid triangle.

Stability Analysis

The number of degrees of freedom must equals the sum of the number of struts and tendons plus the external constraints.

Equilibrium of Nodes

As long as a node is loaded by only two unknown forces, they can be determined using the translational equation of equilibrium for the node.

Equilibrium of Sections

As long as it is possible to make a section on which only three unknown forces act, it is convenient to write the 3 eq. of equilibrium for the section with respect to rotation about the points in which the unknown forces meet.

Design Considerations

Most important is the problem of lateral stability of compressed members and consequently a proper design of a stiffening system.
