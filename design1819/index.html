Exam

There is a single exam for the whole course, consisting in a thorough review of your project, including the structural design.
The structural design must be documented in a consistent manner, comprising at least

  • a technical report on the building, complete with the detailed design of structural members,
  • executive drawings of the load-resisting structure,
  • a few detailed drawings of selected structural members.
