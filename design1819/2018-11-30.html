Reinforced Concrete

In the first part we discussed the materials that form r.c., i.e., concrete and steel and the problem of durability of reinforced concrete.
In the second part we discussed the verification and design procedures for columns subjected to axial forces and beams subjected to bending moments.

The third part was intended for shear forces and the combination of bending and axial forces, but due to time restraints we couldn't discuss these topics. The relevant material is however present in the slides.
