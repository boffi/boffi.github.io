Exam

There is a single exam for the whole course, consisting in a thorough review of your project, including the structural design.
The structural design must be documented in a consistent manner, comprising at least a technical report on the building, complete with the detailed design of a few structural members, and executive drawings of the structure and a few structural members.

Recommended text books

As far as I can tell, the Campus Library has a few copies of "Structures" but very few when compared to your number.
Toniolo's book being quite new, I don't think it is already available from the Library.
IMO "Structures" is a good book for your self-study if you are curious of its subject...
Toniolo's is an in-depth text book that you may like better if you want to become a good designer of reinforced concrete structures because it is both wider and deeper if compared to what we will see in the course.
