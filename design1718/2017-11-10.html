Loadings

Architectural structures must be designed to resist (mechanically or otherwise) different type of loadings with an appropriate level of safety.

We first recognized the need for a characterization of actions and resistances in terms of stochastic variables.

Next we recognized the need to associate different levels of safety to different limit states.

Eventually we devised an approximate method to achieve our aims, the method of partial factors.

The different type of loadings, as defined by the Eurocode and the current Italian building code, were described in the last part of the class.
