Dynamics of Structures

2017-09-25

Forces

Most of this class has been devoted to forces: their composition and decomposition, especially in terms of orthogonal components.

Equilibrium

Equilibrium has been studied in terms of 2D systems, when we study the equilibrium of each part of a discrete body it is convenient to introduce the internal force components, axial force, shear force and bending moment.

Stresses and deformations

The concepts of stress and deformation have been introduced with purely axially loaded members.

Behaviour of Materials

Elastic and non elastic behaviours od axially loaded members has been described, as well as fragile and ductile behaviours and their implications in terms of structural design.
