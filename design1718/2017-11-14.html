Reinforced Concrete Materials

Reinforced concrete is the most used construction material, made of the combination of concrete (an artificial stone) that is good in resisting compressive stresses and steel bars, that resist tensile stresses. The materials of reinforced material are sensitive to environmental agents that lead to concrete degradation and corrosion of steel bars --- these effects can be minimized by proper detailing of the structure.
